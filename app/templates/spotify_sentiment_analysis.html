Spotify Sentiment Analysis


Learn more about the sentiment of your favourite tracks.

To access these values, we use the Spotify API.

We do not store any of your personal information (e.g. how you pay for your Spotify). But to limit the number of API calls we make, we securely store the name and the artist of your favourite songs, as well as your Spotify user ID.


For this analysis, you need to have songs in your liked playlist.
